How to request:
This portal lets you request certificates and schedule appointments with the Barangay office.

 How to Book:

Step 1: Click "Set an Appointment" and choose a date/time.
Step 2: Fill in your personal details and select your preferred date.
Step 3: Confirm your appointment and bring original IDs to your visit.

Required Documents:
Valid photo ID (e.g., government ID, passport)
Proof of address (utility bill, lease, or Barangay certificate)
Any supporting form specific to the service

Processing & Timing: Most requests are processed within 1–3 business days. Emergency or same‑day requests may be subject to availability.

Fees: Small administrative fees may apply depending on the service.
